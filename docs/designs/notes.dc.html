Decision record — archive is the default shelf; delete & unlink stay deliberate
Archive, not deleteThe browser offers a = archive/unarchive (reversible, dims in place) and never a destroy. DELETE /notes/:id (delete_note) exists but has no face — a study note is shelved, not shredded. A hard delete, if it lands, is a confirmed :note delete reading “delete (permanent),” visibly distinct from a.
Unlink is a book-detachPATCH /notes/:id/unlink (unlink_note) severs the book anchor while keeping the note — a different intent from archiving. It lives on the detail read as u · detach from book, never conflated with a.
$EDITOR for proseLong-form bodies (and week-planning's retro reflections) open $VISUAL then $EDITOR (git-commit precedence) — never a rebuilt rich editor in ratatui. Writing the buffer is the save; a quit-without-write keeps the draft. The one-line thought stays in the overlay.
Content-first, title derivedThe title is lifted from the first non-empty line (derive_title_content); full text kept verbatim in content. A PATCH that omits anchors leaves existing citations untouched — only sending anchors replaces them.
